Explore Lekki Phase 1

Discover the vibrant neighborhood, iconic landmarks, and world-class amenities that make Lekki Phase 1 Lagos' most desirable address.

Lekki-Ikoyi Link Bridge

Nigeria's most photographed landmark. This 1.36km cable-stayed bridge connects Lekki to Ikoyi and is a popular jogging spot with stunning views.

5 mins drive

Nike Art Gallery

Nigeria's #1 art destination on TripAdvisor. This 4-story gallery features over 7,000 pieces of traditional and contemporary Nigerian art.

8 mins drive

Lekki Conservation Centre

Home to Africa's longest canopy walkway (401m). Experience nature with 360-degree views from 22ft high towers in this urban oasis.

10 mins drive

Circa Lagos

Upscale waterfront dining with panoramic ocean views. Perfect for romantic dinners and business meetings.

Fine Dining

Bay Lounge

Trendy beachfront lounge offering fresh seafood, cocktails, and live music with cool ocean breeze.

Beach Lounge

Club Quilox

Lagos' most famous nightlife destination. High-energy club with international DJs and celebrity sightings.

Nightlife

The Palms Shopping Mall

Lekki's premier shopping destination with 69 stores, modern 6-screen cinema, and ample parking for 1000+ cars.

5 mins drive

Lekki Arts & Crafts Market

Vibrant open-air market featuring traditional Nigerian art, wood carvings, textiles, beadwork, and antiques from the 1930s.

7 mins drive

Circle Mall

Modern shopping complex with electronics, fashion boutiques, grocery stores, and casual dining options.

3 mins drive

Elegushi Royal Beach

Private beach with horse riding, beach volleyball, and fresh seafood grills. Perfect for weekend getaways.

12 mins drive

Landmark Beach

Pristine beach with water sports, beachfront dining, and the iconic Landmark Event Centre for concerts.

8 mins drive

Upbeat Centre

West Africa's largest trampoline park and recreation center. Perfect for families and fitness enthusiasts.
