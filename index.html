Explore Lekki Phase 1

Discover the vibrant neighborhood, iconic landmarks, and world-class amenities.

Lekki-Ikoyi Link Bridge

Nigeria's most photographed landmark. This 1.36km cable-stayed bridge connects Lekki to Ikoyi.

Nike Art Gallery

Nigeria's #1 art destination featuring over 7,000 pieces of traditional and contemporary art.

Lekki Conservation Centre

Home to Africa's longest canopy walkway (401m) with 360-degree views from 22ft high towers.

Circa Lagos

Upscale waterfront dining with panoramic ocean views. Perfect for romantic dinners.

Bay Lounge

Trendy beachfront lounge offering fresh seafood, cocktails, and live music.

Club Quilox

Lagos' most famous nightlife destination with international DJs.

The Palms Shopping Mall

Lekki's premier shopping destination with 69 stores and modern cinema.

Lekki Arts & Crafts Market

Vibrant open-air market featuring traditional Nigerian art and wood carvings.

Circle Mall

Modern shopping complex with electronics, fashion boutiques, and dining.

Elegushi Royal Beach

Private beach with horse riding, beach volleyball, and fresh seafood grills.

Landmark Beach

Pristine beach with water sports and the iconic Landmark Event Centre.

Upbeat Centre

West Africa's largest trampoline park and recreation center.
